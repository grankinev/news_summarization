The result is empty store shelves, and panicked buyers\n\nThe transportation links that move food around the globe are being tested in unprecedented ways. Shipowners are struggling to change crews and move goods between ports. Airlines have grounded thousands of planes, slashing air freight capacity.\n\nTravel restrictions also are clogging up road networks and making it difficult for farm workers to get where they are needed.
